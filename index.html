SSN TROPHY

SSN trophy is the national level inter collegiate sports festival proudly organised by SSN College of Engineering for the past 17 years. An annual 4 day event, SSN trophy was first held in the year 2003 with participants competing in Basketball, Badminton and Table Tennis. Since then SSN Trophy has grown manifold and has become one of the most desired and appreciated sporting events across the country.

  • SSN trophy has a footfall over 1000 participants
  • 50+ Institutions from over 5 states across India
  • 13 events conducted across 8 sports
  • Cash Prizes and Awards worth Rs. 5 Lakhs

Over the years the Opening and closing ceremonies have been graced by various Indian Sportspersons such as Mr.Sharath Kamal, Mr.John Wright, Ms.Anita Paul, Shri.Robin Singh.We would proudly say that SSN Trophy has been a stepping stone for many currently successful sportspersons from other colleges have participated in SSN Trophy on their way to professional glory. This edition of the SSN Trophy is the edition and it is more special because it is the first year of the decade, we hope that it reaches a whole new level of grandeur, competition and excellence in sports.

General Rules

  1. Selection of participants will be based on merit. Only limited entries for all the sports &games and each institution can send only one team per event.
  2. Only regular bonafide UG & PG students are allowed to represent their college/university team. Age of the player should be below 23 years.
  3. Born on or after 01.07.1995 are eligible to play this tournament. Age proof is mandatory for participation of this tournament.
  4. Pre degree students and casual students are not allowed to participate in this tournament.
  5. All participants are requested to carry their college identity cards for all the games. Those failing to produce the ID cards will not be allowed to participate.
  6. Teams are requested to report half an hour before the scheduled commencement of the game. The participants should wear proper sports wears.
  7. Organising Secretary's decision will be final on all matters concerning the organization and conduct of the tournament.
  8. Unfurnished accommodation will be provided for outstation teams on prior request only and Rs.200/- will be charged for food and accommodation per person per day (optional).
  9. In case of protest, the participant must submit necessary documents required for clarification.
  10. Cash prizes & certificates for first four places and trophies will be awarded for Winners and Runners only. The overall prize money awarded will be 5 lakhs.
  11. Balls shall be provided for Cricket, Table tennis, Squash, Tennis and Badminton.
  12. Online registration is also available at www.ssntrophy.com.
  13. The teams who receive confirmation email only are eligible to participate in the respective event.
  14. The entry form should reach on or before 15.02.2020

Note:- If any team is found violating the rules, the whole institution will be disqualified and participation here in future will be discouraged.
